Rules

  • Players alternate turns
  • Players choose a column with available space to place a piece in the lowest available slot
  • The first player to get four of their pieces in a row wins the game
  • The four in a row can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal
  • If the board is full and neither player has four in a row, the game is a tie
